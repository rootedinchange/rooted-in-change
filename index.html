Meet Our Founder

Natalie Kasten - Miss Missouri Earth 2025

As Miss Missouri Earth 2025 and author of "The Roots That Changed the World," I've witnessed firsthand the incredible impact young people can have when connected with meaningful volunteer opportunities.

Through my work in youth leadership and community service, I've seen a troubling trend: while community needs continue to grow, the volunteer base is rapidly aging. That's why I created Rooted in Change ‚Äì to bridge this critical gap.
